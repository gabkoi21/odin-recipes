Main Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricatta Cheese
  • Mozzarela
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

These are the steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles: Boil the noodles if using traditional ones, then drain and set aside.
  3. Cook the meat: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, cooking until soft. Add ground beef (or sausage) and cook until
  4. Add tomato sauce: Stir in the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, and any optional vegetables like mushrooms or spinach. Simmer for 10-15 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Prepare ricotta mixture: In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, eggs, parsley, and half the Parmesan cheese. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Top with Parmesan: Sprinkle the remaining Parmesan cheese on top of the final layer.
